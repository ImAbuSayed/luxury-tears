Gallan teriyan ch, main taan kho gaya haan
Tere zehreelay ishq ch, poora rull gaya haan
Vairiyan nu vi main, raah ton hata dawangaa
Je tu kahe taan main, ambraan nu la dawangaa
Tere gusse vich vi mainu, disda ae pyaar
Sadde rishte da hunda, roz nava takraar
Aaja mere seene naal, lag ja tu sohniye
Tainu main banawaan apni, umraan di mohniye
